## work

*/wɜːrk/*

> Activity involving mental or physical effort done in order to achieve a purpose or result.

**工作**

- She has been working hard on the project all week.
- 她这整个星期都在努力工作，完成这个项目。

*Work can be both a countable (a work of art) and an uncountable noun (do some work).*

## tune

*/tjuːn/*

- I can't get that tune out of my head!
- 我无法把那个曲调从我的脑海里赶走！

## refine

*/rɪˈfaɪn/*

## shine

/* /ʃaɪn/ */

> To emit light; to be bright by reflection.

**发光；照耀**

- The sun began to shine, melting the snow.
- 太阳开始发光，融化了积雪。
