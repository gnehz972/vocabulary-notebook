## seven

/*ˈsevn̩/*

> The number equivalent to the sum of six and one; one more than six.

**七**

- I have seven apples in my basket.
- 我的篮子里有七个苹果。

*Seven is often associated with luck or completion in various cultures.*

## two

**二**

- 我有两只猫。

*Avoid using "twos" as a plural noun. Use "pairs" or "couples" instead.*

## one

*/wʌn/*

> The number 1; a single thing or unit; a person.

**一; 一个; 人**

- There is only one apple left in the basket.
- 篮子里只剩下一个苹果了。

* "One" can be used as a pronoun to replace a noun, often implying a general or unspecified person. For example, "One should always be polite."
