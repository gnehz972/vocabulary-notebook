## nice

/*naɪs*/

> Pleasant, agreeable, or satisfactory.

**好的; 令人愉快的**

- It was nice to meet you.
- 很高兴认识你。

*Note that "nice" can sometimes be a vague compliment. Consider specifying what was "nice" about something for more impact.*
