## one

*/wʌn/*

> The number 1; a single thing or unit; a person.

**一; 一个; 人**

- There is only one apple left in the basket.
- 篮子里只剩下一个苹果了。

* "One" can be used as a pronoun to replace a noun, often implying a general or unspecified person. For example, "One should always be polite."

## three

/*θriː/*

> The number equivalent to the sum of one and two; a symbol representing this number (3).

**三**

- I have three cats.
- 我有三只猫。

*Note that "three" can also be used as an adjective describing something that has three parts or elements.*
