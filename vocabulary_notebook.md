## competency

/ˈkɒmpɪtənsi/

> The ability to do something successfully or efficiently.

**能力；胜任**

- The company is looking for candidates with a high level of competency in data analysis.
- 公司正在寻找具有较高数据分析能力水平的候选人。

*Competency is often used in a professional context to describe the skills and abilities required for a specific job or role.*


---

## cultivation

*/ˌkʌltɪˈveɪʃən/*

> The act of preparing and working land in order to grow crops or plants; also, the process of trying to develop or improve something.

**耕种；培养**

- The cultivation of rice requires a lot of water.
- 水稻的耕种需要大量的水。

*Cultivation can refer to both literal farming and the figurative development of skills or relationships.*
