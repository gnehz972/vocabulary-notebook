## four

*/fɔːr/*

> The number equivalent to the sum of one and three; one more than three.

**四**

- There are four seasons in a year.
- 一年有四个季节。

*Some people consider the number four unlucky in certain cultures, similar to the number thirteen in Western cultures.*

## three

/*θriː/*

> The number equivalent to the sum of one and two; a symbol representing this number (3).

**三**

- I have three cats.
- 我有三只猫。

*Note that "three" can also be used as an adjective describing something that has three parts or elements.*
