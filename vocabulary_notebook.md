## meet

/miːt/

> To encounter (someone or something) unexpectedly or by arrangement; to come together.

**遇见; 会面**

- I will meet you at the library at 3 PM.
- 我下午三点在图书馆和你见面。

*Often used to describe a planned or unplanned encounter.*

## nice

/*naɪs*/

> Pleasant, agreeable, or satisfactory.

**好的; 令人愉快的**

- It was nice to meet you.
- 很高兴认识你。

*Note that "nice" can sometimes be a vague compliment. Consider specifying what was "nice" about something for more impact.*
