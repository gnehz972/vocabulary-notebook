## archetype

/*ˈɑːrkɪtaɪp/*

> A very typical example of a certain person or thing; an original model or pattern from which other similar things are copied.

**原型**

- The hero in most adventure movies is an archetype: brave, selfless, and determined.
- 大部分冒险电影中的英雄都是一个原型：勇敢、无私、且有决心。

*Archetype is often used in literature and psychology to describe recurring symbols, characters, or situations across different works and cultures.*
